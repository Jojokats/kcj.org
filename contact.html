Where is Kids Code Jeunesse?

Our head office is located in Montréal, Quebec. Our instructors are based in BC, Ontario, and in Québec.

Thanks to the amazing support of Vigilant Global, Kids Code Jeunesse has an office at the Notman House, a historical building in the heart of Montréal, which today houses the cutting edge of Montréal’s start up community.
