Where is Kids Code Jeunesse?

Our head office is located in Montréal, Quebec. Our instructors are based in BC, Ontario, and in Québec.

Email:

If you are a teacher or a community leader: educators@kidscodejeunesse.org
If you are a parent: parents@kidscodejeunesse.org
If you are interested in volunteering or are already a volunteer: volunteers@kidscodejeunesse.org
For all other inquiries: contact@kidscodejeunesse.org

Newsletter:

Want to receive news from us on what’s coming up and what’s been happening with computer programming in Canada – for you and your children? Sign up for our Canadian newsletter!

Our Head Office:

Thanks to the amazing support of Vigilant Global, Kids Code Jeunesse has an office at the Notman House, a historical building in the heart of Montréal, which today houses the cutting edge of Montréal’s start up community.
