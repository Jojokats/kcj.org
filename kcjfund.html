the kids code jeunesse fund

Help us build the Fund so we can provide trained computer programming instructors to teach in the classroom, as well as to train educators so they can bring computer programming to their students. The Fund is set to support every student and educator to learn to code in our underprivileged schools and community centers.


become a sponsor


The Kids Code Jeunesse Fund exists for Canadian schools and community centres who do not have the financial resources to bring one of our trained instructors to teach computing to their students. Availability depends on the location and the financial resources. As of Fall 2016, we have lead instructors in BC, Ontario and Quebec.


We reach out to companies and individuals to help build and maintain the Fund.


Tax receipts are available for all donations $500 +.
