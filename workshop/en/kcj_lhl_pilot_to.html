An introduction to computational thinking and coding in the classroom! On Saturday February 10th, Kids Code Jeunesse in partnership with Lighthouse Labs will provide a workshop for teachers on the basics of coding and computational thinking. This free, full-day workshop will provide K - 12 educators with the tools they need to inspire and encourage the use of technology inside and outside of the classroom. No coding experience? No problem. This workshop is designed for beginners.

Toronto - Saturday, February 10th, 9:00am - 5:00pm
